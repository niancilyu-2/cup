Boston-area watch guide

Where to Watch

Public / Official / Community Watch Parties

1

FIFA Fan Festival™ Boston at City Hall Plaza

City Hall Square / City Hall Plaza, Boston

Free all-ages fan festival, June 12-27, with live broadcasts of multiple Group Stage matches, games, music, live entertainment, local vendors, football-themed activities, food/drinks and cultural programming. Registration required; access limited to 5,000 fans at a time.

2

Boston Fan Zone at The Lawn on D

420 D St, Boston

Free fan zone from June 13-July 9 with live broadcasts, music, food, culture and community programming.

3

Fenway FC at The Green at 401 Park

401 Park Dr, Boston

Free outdoor watch parties, June 12-July 19, including Team USA watch parties, large LED screen, outdoor seating, soccer-themed games, activations, food/drinks, retail pop-ups and family-friendly programming.

4

Mayor Wu's Watch Party: Spain vs. Cabo Verde

Town Field, 1565 Dorchester Ave, Dorchester

Free city watch party for Spain vs. Cabo Verde, June 15 at 1 p.m.; large screen, live music, family activities, face painting, food vendors and community programming.

5

Mayor Wu's Watch Party: Brazil vs. Haiti

Parkman Bandstand, Boston Common, 139 Tremont St, Boston

Free city watch party for Brazil vs. Haiti, June 19 at 9 p.m.; large screen, music, food vendors and family-friendly activities.

6

Mayor Wu's Watch Party: Colombia vs. Portugal

East Boston Memorial Stadium, 143 Porter St, East Boston

Free city watch party for Colombia vs. Portugal, June 27 at 7:30 p.m.; large screen, live music, activities, food vendors and community programming.

7

Cambridge United - Where the World Comes Together

Central Square, Harvard Square / Brattle St, Inman Square, MIT Open Space / Kendall Square, Donnelly Field and Cambridge Crossing

Seven free outdoor Cambridge watch events, including Haiti vs. Scotland, Iraq vs. Norway, Scotland vs. Morocco, England vs. Ghana, Norway vs. France, a Round of 32 match and a Quarter-final.

8

Kulture Festival

420 D St, Seaport District, Boston

Haitian community celebration with performances, comedy, music, food and a screening of Haiti vs. Scotland.

9

Haiti vs. Scotland Watch Party at MCEC

415 Summer St, Boston

Haiti vs. Scotland watch party with soccer, music, food and celebration led by Boston's Haitian and Scottish communities.

10

Mattapan World Cup: Family Fun Day

Hunt-Almont Playground, 40 Almont St, Mattapan

Free Haiti-focused family celebration with live DJs, food/drinks, local vendors, games for all ages, culture and community pride.

11

Home Ground at The Haven

284 Amory St, Jamaica Plain

Three-day Scotland-focused football festival, June 12-14, celebrating Scottish supporter culture; streams Scotland matches live with live music, food trucks, whisky tastings and more.

12

Scotland House Boston at The Anchor

The Anchor Boston, Charlestown Navy Yard / Shipyard Park, Boston

Scotland House hub with Scotland-focused party days, match viewing, food/drink and fan culture programming.

13

Revere Field of Play Watch Parties

Various locations, Revere

Free outdoor Revere watch-party series starting with Canada vs. Bosnia and USA vs. Paraguay, plus later events in neighborhoods/business districts with food trucks, local vendors and family-friendly surprises.

14

Fiesta de Futbol Watch Parties

Chelsea Square, Chelsea

Chelsea community watch parties with large-screen match broadcasts, live music, local food vendors, community soccer programming and cultural celebrations.

15

MetroWest Regional Fan Zone - Marlborough

Kelleher Field, Marlborough

Opening-weekend fan zone, June 11-13, showing multiple matches including Mexico vs. South Africa, with rain-or-shine festival programming.

16

MetroWest Regional Fan Zone - Franklin

Franklin Town Common and Hayward Manor, Franklin

June 24-25 fan zone featuring Switzerland vs. Canada, Scotland vs. Brazil, Ecuador vs. Germany and Tunisia vs. Netherlands, plus vendors, music and activities.
